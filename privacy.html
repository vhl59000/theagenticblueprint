Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026  ·  GDPR-compliant

1. Overview

The Agentic Blueprint (https://www.theagenticblueprint.com) is operated by Valentin Henry-Léo. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

TL;DR: We collect only what's necessary to process your purchase and deliver your product. We don't sell your data. We don't track you with advertising cookies. Simple.

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data, only what is necessary for order processing and delivery:

Email address Collected at checkout via Stripe. Used to deliver your PDF and provide support.
Payment information Processed entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card details.
Purchase record Product purchased, date, and amount — for order management and refund processing.
IP address Collected by Vercel (our hosting provider) for security and abuse prevention. Not used for advertising.

We do not collect:

  • No advertising or behavioral tracking cookies
  • No third-party analytics (Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, etc.)
  • No social media tracking
  • No device fingerprinting

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Order fulfillment: Delivering your PDF guide via email.
  • Customer support: Responding to your questions and resolving issues.
  • Fraud prevention: Detecting and preventing fraudulent purchases.
  • Legal obligations: Complying with applicable tax and financial regulations.

We do not send marketing emails unless you have explicitly opted in. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Payment Processing via Stripe

All transactions are processed by Stripe, Inc., a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. When you purchase a product, you are subject to Stripe's privacy practices in addition to ours.

Stripe may collect and process your payment data in accordance with their Privacy Policy. We receive only a transaction confirmation and your email address — never your full card details.

5. Cookies

This website uses only strictly necessary cookies required for the checkout process to function (e.g., session management via Stripe). We do not use:

  • Advertising or tracking cookies
  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Social media cookies (Facebook, Twitter, etc.)

Because we only use strictly necessary cookies, we are not required to display a cookie consent banner under GDPR. If we ever add non-essential cookies, we will obtain your consent first.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary:

  • Email address & purchase records: Retained for 3 years for accounting and legal compliance, then deleted.
  • Support correspondence: Retained for 12 months after your last interaction, then deleted.

You can request earlier deletion by contacting us (subject to legal retention obligations — we may need to keep minimal financial records).

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Right of rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"): Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to restriction of processing: Request that we limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: Object to processing of your personal data for legitimate interests.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at valentin@theagenticblueprint.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority (in France: the CNIL).

8. Data Transfers

Your data may be processed outside the EU/EEA when using Stripe (US-based) or Vercel (US-based hosting). Both providers offer appropriate safeguards under GDPR through Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent mechanisms.
